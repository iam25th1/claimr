Tour fix batch
================================

What was broken and why

The "?" icon in the navbar called startTour() which sets shared state to
"show step 0". But the TourOverlay component is only mounted on the
/dashboard/discover route. Clicking "?" from the landing page therefore
set state but rendered nothing visible until you also navigated to
/discover. From the user perspective: clicking the icon did nothing.

Separately, when "Take the tour" was clicked from the welcome modal,
race conditions between the modal unmounting and the target element
finishing layout could leave the spotlight with no measurable target,
so it rendered a centered tooltip with no highlighted area.


What this batch ships

  Renamed Launch App to Explore
    Single navbar CTA now reads "Explore" regardless of auth state.
    Cleaner and matches what the button actually does.

  "?" button now auto-navigates
    Clicking the help-circle icon from anywhere now calls startTour()
    AND routes to /dashboard/discover if you're not there. The tour
    overlay always has somewhere to render.

  Visible Take a tour button on the Explore page
    Top right of the page, next to the page header. Sparkle icon,
    "Take a tour" label, restarts the tour from step 1. Way more
    discoverable than the small ? icon.

  Overlay polls for target element
    Instead of measuring once and giving up, the overlay now polls
    every 80ms for up to 3.2 seconds until the target both exists
    AND has non-zero dimensions. Fixes the timing flake where
    spotlights would land on an unmeasured element. Resize and
    scroll watchers stay wired up the entire time the step is active.
