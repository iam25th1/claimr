Auth fix v3 - returning user flow

Two bugs that combine to send signed-in users through onboarding every
time they hit the landing page:

  1. Hero CTAs ("I'm a Creator" / "I'm a Project") always linked to
     /onboarding, regardless of auth state.
  2. /onboarding only redirected authenticated users when mode=signin.
     For signup mode (the default, what the Hero CTAs use), it fast-
     forwarded them to the Wallet ready step instead of dashboard.

Fixes:

  components/claimr/hero.tsx                REBUILT
    Detects auth state. When authenticated, swaps the CTAs to
    "Go to creator dashboard" -> /dashboard/discover
    "Go to project dashboard" -> /project
    When signed out, keeps the original creator/project signup CTAs.

  components/claimr/onboarding-cards.tsx    PATCHED
    The "bounce authenticated user away" effect now fires for any
    authenticated user, not just mode=signin. Initial step state
    also no longer fast-forwards to wallet for authenticated visitors.


What returning users will experience after this

  Visit /                  Hero shows "Go to creator dashboard" + "Go to project dashboard"
  Click either             Lands in that dashboard, no detour
  Visit /onboarding        Bounced immediately to dashboard
  Visit /onboarding?role=X Bounced to /dashboard or /project
  Click Launch App         Already auth-aware from earlier batch, goes to dashboard
