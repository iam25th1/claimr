What's different

Old onboarding: one screen, email input, immediate router.replace to
dashboard. No funding step, no profile step, no progress indicator.

New onboarding: real 4-or-5 step flow with progress dots at top:

  1. Role pick (only shown when ?role= isn't in URL)
     Two cards side by side. Tap one, advances.

  2. Email entry
     With a Circle-wallet explainer callout, "no MetaMask" message.
     Submits via signUp(), advances to verifying.

  3. Verifying
     Spinner + rotating messages while Circle handles OTP and wallet
     provisioning. Auto-advances when user.walletAddress lands.

  4. Wallet ready
     Shows the new wallet address with copy button + Arcscan link.
     Shows USDC balance, live, refetching every 5 seconds so a faucet
     drop lands visibly. Fund Wallet button opens existing modal.
     Continue button to advance, with copy that adapts to funded state.

  5. Profile (optional, skippable)
     Name + X handle + bio. Saves to localStorage profile store.
     Skip and they go straight to dashboard.

Hero CTAs on landing already pass ?role=creator or ?role=project, so
the role step is skipped for users coming from those links. Anyone
hitting /onboarding without a role param sees the role picker first.
