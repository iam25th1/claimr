================================
TOUR BEHAVIOR
================================

First visit (any browser, localStorage empty):
  - User lands on /dashboard/discover
  - Welcome modal appears centered with backdrop
  - "Take the tour" -> step 0, modal closes, spotlight begins
  - "Skip for now" or close -> sets seen flag, never auto-appears again

Each step:
  - SVG mask dims everything except a rounded rect around the target
    (8px padding, 8px corner radius). Target is fully visible and
    interactive-looking, though clicks are absorbed by the overlay
    to keep the user inside the tour.
  - Pink animated outline pulses around target
  - Tooltip card on whichever side has the most room
  - Step counter: "Step N of 6"
  - Back disabled on step 0
  - Last step shows "Finish" instead of "Next"
  - X button at top-right of tooltip skips and marks seen

Replay:
  - HelpCircle icon in top-right of navbar
  - Click any time -> step 0, runs full tour again
  - Already-seen flag stays set (no re-prompt of welcome modal)
