Tour fix v3 - step 6 taskbar collision
================================

The bug

  Step 6 (Sign in to take action) targeted the sidebar bottom CTA,
  which sits flush against the bottom of the viewport. Its placement
  was "right", which positioned the tooltip beside the target with
  a clamp of vh-220 from the top. That left 220px between the tooltip's
  top edge and the bottom of the viewport, which sounds like enough
  but isn't:

    - The tooltip card for step 6 is ~250px tall (longer body text).
    - If the browser window sits flush with the Windows taskbar,
      vh includes pixels that get visually covered by the taskbar.
    - Result: the Finish/Back buttons at the bottom of the tooltip
      ended up overlapping with the taskbar and were unclickable.


The fix

  Step 6 now uses placement "top". The tooltip floats above the sidebar
  CTA target, which has tons of vertical room since the target itself
  is at the bottom of the screen. Natural reading flow: tooltip up
  top, highlighted button right below it.

  Right and left placements now use a centered-on-target approach
  with constants:
    TOOLTIP_H_ESTIMATE = 240   (conservative card height)
    BOTTOM_SAFE_MARGIN = 64    (clear of taskbar + any chrome)

  Tooltips can never spill within 64px of the bottom of the viewport,
  on any platform, regardless of target position.

  centeredTooltipPos (used when no target is measurable) also picks
  up the new bottom margin so the fallback tooltip stays clear.
